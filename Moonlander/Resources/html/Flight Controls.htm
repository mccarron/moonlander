Instructions for controlling your lander:

Control the thrust by using the throttle slider at the upper right side of the display.

Control the lander roll angle with the large and small control arrows at the middle right of the display.

Your lander instrument panel can only display four instruments at a time.  If you wish to see another instrument, select it from the list on the lower right side of the display (it will begin to blink) then touch control panel where you would like it to be displayed.

The game will remember your successful landings on the lunar surface as well as the crashed landers you have left there.  Be careful not to burn any flags with the flame from your descent engine.

Shake the tablet to bring up a dialog to end the game early. Don't forget to visit the only McDonalds on the moon for a special treat.
