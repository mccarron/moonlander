Moonlander has four unique challenges to pit your lunar lander piloting skills against your peers in Game Center.

 

Time Challenge

Land successfully in the shortest time.

 

 

Fuel Challenge

Land successfully using the least fuel.

 

 

Distance Challenge

Land successfully as far as possible from the first lunar McDonalds. Think left if you wish to do well in this challenge.

 

 

Cheeseburger Challenge

You start with 1000 points and are penalized depending on your landing, remaining fuel, and the distance from McDonalds.  You must land in view of McDonalds to earn a score in this challenge.
